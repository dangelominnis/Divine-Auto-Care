What Does Maintenance Include?

● The exterior of your vehicle will receive a thorough wash.
● Deep clean wheels and tires.
● Clay front end and mirrors to free bugs off the paint.
● Clean exhaust tips.
● Rubber floor mats removed and scrubbed.
● Rinse and blow out all cracks and dry vehicles with non abrasive microfiber towels.
● Basic sealant that protects the paint for 1-2 months.
● Light Interior vacuum.
● Dash, console and cup holders cleaned.
● Interior and exterior glass cleaned.
● Wipe down plastics.
● Wipe Door Jambs.
● Dress Tires.

This package is best suited for customers who are maintaining a new vehicle or have received one of our full detail packages recently. Recommended weekly,biweekly or monthly. We provide discounts for our valued customers who bring us multiple vehicles at home or workplace.
